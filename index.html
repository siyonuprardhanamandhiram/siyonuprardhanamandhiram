Servants of the Most High

Sis. Daya Mani

Choir Lead & Ministry

Sis. Daya Mani is a pillar of spiritual strength at Siyonu Prardhana Mandhiram. With a heart fully surrendered to the Holy Spirit, she has been leading the congregation in worship for over a decade. Her ministry is not just about singing, but about ushering in the presence of God where yokes are broken and hearts are healed.

Beyond the choir, she plays a vital role in mentoring young women in the church and organizing community outreach programs in Yernagudem. Her dedication to the gospel and her compassionate leadership inspire everyone around her.
