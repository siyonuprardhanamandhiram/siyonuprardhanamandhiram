Welcome Home

Grace, Mercy, and Peace unto you

Welcome to Siyonu Prardhana Mandhiram in Yernagudem. We are a community defined not by perfection, but by the transforming grace of Jesus Christ. We believe the church is a family where every person is valued, loved, and called to a higher purpose in His Kingdom.

As we celebrate the miracle of Christmas, our deepest prayer is that you would encounter the living God in a tangible way. We are driven by a vision to see every life transformed by the Gospel. Whether you are navigating a season of abounding joy or walking through a valley of shadow, you will find a supportive community here dedicated to walking alongside you.

Our Leadership

Servants of the Most High God

Choir Lead & Ministry Pillar

Sis. Daya Mani

With over a decade of faithful, consecrated service, Sis. Daya Mani is a foundational pillar at Siyonu Prardhana Mandhiram. Her ministry is defined by a deep sensitivity to the Holy Spirit and an unwavering passion for biblical truth. She leads the congregation not just in songs, but into the very Holy of Holies, fostering an atmosphere where yokes are broken and hearts are healed.

Her influence extends far beyond the pulpit; she is a spiritual mother to many, mentoring young women and cultivating an environment where the gifts of the Spirit can flourish in the next generation of believers.
